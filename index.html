Hannah Pezzack is a British writer, editor and DJ who is presently based in the Netherlands. Having graduated with a research master's in Cultural Analysis from the University of Amsterdam, her work pertains to the multi-nuances and dispersed politics of intimacies and sexuality. In particular, Hannah is drawn to discourses surrounding eco-sexuality, eroticism and nonhuman entanglements. She has authored research papers on the materiality of dildos and sadomasochistic mating rituals of insects, as well as a study of Anne Imhofâ€™s videowork ONE (2020) for Rooftop Press. As the managing editor of Extra Extra, a printed cultural journal and online platform focusing on sensuality and urban life, she edited the words of Lauren Berlant, Franco "Bifo'' Berardi, McKenzie Wark, Legacy Russell and Huw Lemmey, amongst many others. Also attuned to sonic encounters, Hannah is a regular contributor to record labels, festivals and magazines, including The Wire, Ransom Note, Resident Advisor, Worms, The Quietus, Sonic Acts, Uncloud, and Dekmantel.
